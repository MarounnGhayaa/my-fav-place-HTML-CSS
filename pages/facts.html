Why I love Kobayat

  • Natural Beauty:
    • The mountainous terrain, snowy winters, and scenic views in Kobayat make it a wonderful place to connect with nature. Whether it's hiking, skiing, or just enjoying the landscapes, the natural surroundings can evoke a deep sense of peace and belonging.

  • Historical and Cultural Significance:
    • Kobayat's long history, dating back thousands of years, filled with ancient ruins from the Phoenician, Greek, and Roman periods, likely gives you a sense of pride and connection to the past. Its historical depth can foster a sense of admiration for its enduring beauty and cultural richness.

  • Unified community:
    • Kobayat has a strong sense of community. Its people are known for their warmth and hospitality, which can make you feel at home and appreciated whenever you visit.

  • Peaceful Environment:
    • The relatively small population and tranquil atmosphere of Kobayat, especially in the winter months when many leave the mountains, might provide a calm and serene environment where you can escape the noise of everyday life. This sense of calm can be something you cherish.

  • Activities:
    • The outdoor activities like hiking, mountain biking, skiing, and camping offer a sense of adventure and connection to nature that could make you feel more alive and in tune with your surroundings. Engaging in these activities in a place like Kobayat could give you a sense of freedom and joy.

  • A Place to Relax and Reconnect:
    • Kobayat might be a place where you can disconnect from the fast pace of modern life and reconnect with simpler, quieter moments. This feeling of relaxation and tranquility can make you feel a deep connection to the village.


Extra info: The Lebanese northern village of Kobayat was voted as Lebanon's "Favorite Village" in the L'Orient Le Jour (OLJ) 2019 competition, winning over nine other nominated Lebanese villages selected to compete: Amchit, Barouk, Baskinta, Becharre, Cana, Ghazir, Hebbariye, Hermel, and Machghara.
